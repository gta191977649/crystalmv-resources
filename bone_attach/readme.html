Bone attachments

Bone attachments is a script for Multi Theft Auto: San Andreas. It provides you with scripting functions for attaching elements to bones of peds and players. These functions are described in bone_attach/functions.txt file.

A simple example:

ped = createPed(105,0,0,3)
burger = createObject(2880,0,0,0)
exports.bone_attach:attachElementToBone(burger,ped,12,0,0,0,0,-90,0)

It creates a ped in the map center and attaches a burger to his hand.
